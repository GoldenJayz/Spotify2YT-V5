S2YT

Home

S2YT is a website that allows you to convert your Spotify playlists to YouTube playlists. This is useful if you want to listen to your Spotify playlists on a device that doesn't support Spotify, such as a car stereo.

Convert

To convert your Spotify playlist to a YouTube playlist, simply follow the steps below:

  1. Go to your Spotify playlist
  2. Copy the playlist name
  3. Paste into input box
  4. Click "Convert"
Enter the Spotify playlist name.
